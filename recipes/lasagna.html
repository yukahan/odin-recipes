lasagna (lie)

Description

Lasagna is good. Lasagna is for me. Lasagna is for everybody.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or more to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package Polish sausage, or to taste, cut into 1-inch squares
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  • Step 2: Cook and stir sausage, onion, and green bell pepper
  • Step 3: until vegetables start to become tender, about 8 minutes
